What is Moon Water?

Moon water is water that has been charged with the energy of the moon. This ancient practice involves placing water in a location where it can absorb the moon's rays, typically during a full moon or other significant lunar phases.

The moon's energy is subtle yet powerful, influencing tides, emotions, and spiritual energy. By creating moon water, you are capturing this lunar essence and making it available for use in your daily spiritual practice.

How to Create Moon Water

A step-by-step guide to crafting your lunar elixir

1

Gather Your Materials

  • Clean glass jar or bottle (clear glass works best)
  • Spring water, distilled water, or collected rainwater
  • Optional: Crystals, herbs, or written intentions
  • Cleansing tool (sage, palo santo, or sound cleansing)
2

Cleanse Your Space

Begin by cleansing your space and materials. Light sage or palo santo and smudge your jar, water, and the area where you'll be working. You can also use sound (bell, singing bowl) or visualization.

3

Set Your Intention

Hold the water in your hands and speak your intention clearly. Write it on paper and place it under the jar, or simply infuse the water with your energy through focused thought.

"I charge this water with the moon's healing energy for emotional balance and inner peace."

"May this water carry the moon's wisdom and guide me toward my highest good."

4

Place in Moonlight

Place your jar outside where it will receive direct moonlight. Windowsills work well if you can't place it outside. Leave it overnight, from moonrise to moonset if possible.

Placement Tips:

  • Choose a safe, stable location
  • Avoid areas with artificial light pollution
  • Crystal grids or altars enhance the charging
  • Trust your intuition about placement
5

Collect at Dawn

Retrieve your moon water before the sun rises. Thank the moon for its gift and bring the water inside. Store it in a cool, dark place or refrigerator.

6

Use with Gratitude

Use your moon water mindfully. Drink it, add it to baths, use it in rituals, or share it with plants. Always use it with intention and gratitude.

Enhanced Moon Water Rituals

Full Moon Manifestation Ritual

Materials needed: Moon water, crystals, journal, candle

  1. Create a sacred space with your moon water in the center
  2. Write your intentions on paper, focusing on what you want to manifest
  3. Place crystals around the jar to amplify the energy
  4. Speak your intentions aloud while holding the jar
  5. Leave the setup overnight to charge under the full moon
  6. The next day, drink the water and keep the intentions nearby

New Moon Intention Setting

Materials needed: Moon water, pen, paper, optional crystals

  1. Begin in a quiet, darkened space
  2. Write down what you wish to release and what you wish to invite
  3. Place the paper under the moon water jar
  4. Hold the jar and visualize your intentions coming to fruition
  5. Leave overnight to charge with new moon energy
  6. Use the water throughout the lunar cycle to stay connected to your intentions
