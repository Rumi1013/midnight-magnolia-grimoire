Our Story

From ancestral roots to digital innovation, discover how Midnight Magnolia is transforming art, design, and ancestral wisdom into sustainable income streams.

Meet Latisha Vincent-Waters

Visionary entrepreneur, artist, and technology advocate dedicated to empowering Black women through digital innovation and ancestral wisdom.

The Magnolia Legacy

Like the mighty magnolia tree that withstands Southern storms while blooming in quiet strength, Midnight Magnolia represents resilience, beauty, and transformation. Our name honors the Southern heritage that shaped us while embracing the digital future we're building.

From Art to Innovation

With a background in art and design, Latisha recognized the transformative power of technology when she discovered AI tools that could amplify creative work. This sparked a mission to bridge the digital divide for Black women entrepreneurs, providing accessible technology education and practical business tools.

The Technology Empowerment Mission

Midnight Magnolia exists to transform how Black women engage with technology. We believe that when Black women have access to AI literacy, digital tools, and business automation, we can create sustainable income streams that honor our ancestral wisdom while building generational wealth.
